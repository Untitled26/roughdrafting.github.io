Premise:

Meet the Gang. Introduce a story about making real friendships and opening your life up to the people around you.
Thus I determined a character to base this series around is a secluded, controlling, sarcastic logic monster named Lynn.
The series begins during move in day of university, where Lynn meets her roommate, her counterpart, named Chloe. A codependent individual who lacks her own direction and needs people, dragging Lynn into the world of friendships that are the rest of the cast.
Taro, Kate Taylor, Morgan, Jack, Ken, and Eli.
Together this bunch of dysfunctional teenagers face the world of university, Society, Adulthood, Friends, and Family.
