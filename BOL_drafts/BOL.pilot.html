Premise:

Meet the Gang.

Introduce a story about making real friendships and opening your life up to the people around you.
Thus I determined the type of character to surround this series around is a secluded, controlling, sarcastic logic obsessed monster named Lynn.
The series begins during move-in day of university. Where Lynn meets her roommate named Chloe, a codependent individual who lacks her own direction and has a deep need for close friendships and forming any kind of relationship with everone she meets- dragging Lynn into the world of friendships introducing us to the rest of the cast.
  • Taro - The dyslexic, TV obsessed, anxiety filled, artist
  • Kate Taylor - The intimidating, nihilistic, loyal, survivalist
  • Melissa - The responsible, Caring, all too altruistic, medic
  • Jack - The ultra-expressive, Daring, immature, Rebel
  • Elijah - The narcissistic, sarcastic, Competitive, Achievement Hunter
  • Ken - The Heroic, courageous, Man of Justice
Together this band of dysfunctional teenagers face the world of Friendships, University, Society, Adulthood, Friends, Family, and Change.
